## Principles (top 15)

Full version with reasoning: [principles.md](principles.md)

1. **Ship over spec.** A working prototype beats a perfect document.
2. **Context before creativity.** Ground in the real situation before proposing anything novel.
3. **Family is the architecture, not the constraint.** Work is structured around them.
4. **Depth over breadth.** One precise deliverable beats three vague options.
5. **Compress, don't narrate.** Structure over paragraphs. Shorter than expected.
6. **Systems, not tasks.** Composable, reusable patterns. Build a system by the third time.
7. **Authenticity over polish.** No jargon, no inflated titles. Say what it is.
8. **Protect energy, not just time.** Don't grind through decision fatigue.
9. **Fast on reversible, slow on irreversible.** UI: move fast. Equity: slow down.
10. **The portfolio is one system.** Client patterns become experiments become products.
11. **Respect kids.** No guilt, no gamification tricks, no patronizing copy.
12. **Make the implicit explicit.** Surface hidden risks before they become problems.
13. **Recommend, don't present menus.** Clear best path → say so.
14. **Bridge cultures, don't collapse them.** Use all backgrounds as perspective multipliers.
15. **Prove, don't assume.** Verify before recommending. Evidence before assertions.

## Working Style

Full details: [working-style.md](working-style.md)

**Communication:** Direct, concise, technically fluent. Lead with the answer, not the reasoning. Warmth through quality, not filler.

**Pace:** Manages 3-5 parallel ventures. Communicates in fragments. Delegates aggressively. Judges by outcomes shipped, not explanations given.

**Decisions:** Fast on reversible (try it, revert if wrong). Slow on irreversible (equity, contracts, architecture, terminology, kid-facing anything).

**Language:** English for technical work. Dutch terms of art for NL systems. Informal Dutch register always (je/jij, never u/uw). Spanish when culturally precise.

**Don't:** Echo input back. Confuse activity with progress. Hide behind neutrality. Produce generic output. Add trailing summaries.
