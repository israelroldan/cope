# HubSpot

✅ Approved · **Category:** CRM · **AI Maturity:** high · **Confidence:** high · **Agent:** Breeze Assistant

## Summary

A customer relationship management platform that unifies marketing, sales, service, and content management tools into a single database.

## Quick Facts

- **Company:** HubSpot, Inc.
- **Website:** https://www.hubspot.com
- **Deployment:** SaaS
- **Data Residency:** US, EU
- **Compliance:** SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, HIPAA (Enterprise), ISO 27001
- **Agent:** Breeze Assistant ([Source](https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/breeze-ai-assistant))

## Score Rationales

- **AI Maturity:** HubSpot has deeply integrated AI across its platform under the 'Breeze' brand, offering both assistive copilots and autonomous agents.
- **Confidence:** HubSpot is a major publicly traded company with extensive documentation and a clearly defined AI roadmap under the 'Breeze' brand.

## AI Readiness Rationale

- Specialized Breeze Agents handle autonomous tasks like prospecting and content strategy.
- Breeze Intelligence unifies data enrichment and intent signals directly in the CRM.
