# AFAS HR/Payroll

✅ Approved · **Category:** ERP · **AI Maturity:** medium · **Confidence:** high

## Summary

AFAS HR/Payroll is a module within the AFAS ERP suite that manages employee records, automates salary processing, and provides self-service portals for leave and declarations.

## Quick Facts

- **Company:** AFAS Software
- **Website:** https://www.afas.nl
- **Deployment:** SaaS
- **Compliance:** GDPR, ISAE 3402, ISO 27001

## Score Rationales

- **AI Maturity:** Uses AI for specific tasks like document recognition and anomaly detection but relies heavily on rule-based automation.
- **Confidence:** Market leader in the Netherlands with extensive documentation and public feature lists.

## AI Readiness Rationale

- Integrated OCR for expense handling
- Rule-based 'Signals' system acts as automated monitoring
- Focus is on administrative automation rather than generative AI

## Landscape Tags

**Roles:** System Of Record, Workflow, Analytics
**CIB Domains:** Workforce and Skills, Insights and Analytics, Communications and Collaboration

- Acts as the central database for all employee and payroll data.
