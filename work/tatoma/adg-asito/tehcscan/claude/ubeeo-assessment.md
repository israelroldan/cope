# Ubeeo

✅ Approved · **Category:** HR & Workforce · **AI Maturity:** medium · **Confidence:** medium

## Summary

Ubeeo is an applicant tracking system (ATS) that helps recruitment teams manage vacancies, track candidates through the hiring pipeline, and automate communication workflows.

## Quick Facts

- **Company:** Ubeeo
- **Website:** https://www.ubeeo.nl
- **Deployment:** SaaS
- **Data Residency:** Europe
- **Compliance:** GDPR

## Score Rationales

- **AI Maturity:** Integrates generative AI for content creation and parsing algorithms for data entry, but remains primarily a workflow-driven tool.
- **Confidence:** Established Dutch recruitment software with clear feature sets, though deep technical details on specific AI models are less public.

## AI Readiness Rationale

- Uses generative AI to draft and optimize job descriptions.
- Employes parsing technology to structure candidate data from resumes.
- Focuses on process automation rather than autonomous decision making.

## Landscape Tags
