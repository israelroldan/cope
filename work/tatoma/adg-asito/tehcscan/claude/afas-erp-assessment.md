- **Deployment:** SaaS
- **Data Residency:** Netherlands
- **Compliance:** ISO 27001, ISAE 3402 Type II, GDPR (AVG)
- **Agent:** Jonas ([Source](https://www.afas.nl/jonas))

## Score Rationales

- **AI Maturity:** Features a branded AI assistant 'Jonas' with generative capabilities integrated into the ERP suite.
- **Confidence:** Market leader in the Netherlands with extensive public documentation and confirmed AI releases.
