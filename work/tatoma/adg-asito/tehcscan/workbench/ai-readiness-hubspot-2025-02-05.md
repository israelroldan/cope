## AI Limitations (What It CAN'T Do)

### Subscription Requirements

| Feature | Required Tier |
|---------|---------------|
| Content Remix | Content Hub Professional or Enterprise |
| Social post generation | Marketing Hub Professional or Enterprise |
| SMS generation | Marketing SMS or SMS Shortcode add-on |
| Full AI feature access | Professional tier minimum |

### Technical Limitations

- **Sensitive data warning**: Users advised not to share sensitive information in AI prompts
- **AI accuracy disclaimer**: "AI-generated content may be inaccurate" - human review recommended
- **Admin configuration required**: Admins must enable "Give users access to generative AI tools" in settings
- **Regional availability**: Some AI features limited to specific regions
- **Pricing escalation**: Costs increase significantly as contact lists grow

### What Breeze Cannot Do

- Replace human editorial judgment for brand-critical content
- Access external data sources without integration setup
- Perform actions outside HubSpot ecosystem natively
- Generate content in all languages (primary support for major languages)
