## KEY LIMITATIONS AND CONSTRAINTS

- **No AI capabilities:** All automation is rule-based, not intelligent
- **No conversational interface:** Employees cannot query schedules in natural language
- **No predictive analytics:** Cannot forecast demand, absence, or staffing needs
- **External dependency:** Financial data accuracy relies on ERP integration stability
- **Privacy considerations:** Employee location data + precise time tracking requires strict internal policy adherence
- **Mobile limitations:** Planners need tablet/desktop for backend adjustments; no full-featured phone app for planning
- **No content generation:** No AI-assisted report writing, email drafting, or document analysis
