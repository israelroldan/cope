## 2. AI Capabilities & Features

### 2.1 The "Tessa" AI Agent

**Tessa** is the branded conversational interface designed to reduce administrative friction for employees and planners.

* **Function:** Embedded within the Employee Self Service (ESS) mobile app.
* **Capabilities:**
* **Natural Language Interaction:** Employees can chat with Tessa as if messaging a colleague (e.g., *"When is my next shift?"*).
* **Self-Service Actions:** Handles shift swaps, leave requests, and schedule queries autonomously.
* **Availability:** 24/7 support for field staff.


* **Maturity:** Introduced in late 2025; positioned as "The Smart AI Assistant" specifically for reducing helpdesk/HR queries.

### 2.2 Core Optimization & Machine Learning

ORTEC differentiates itself by combining traditional Operations Research (mathematical optimization) with modern Machine Learning (ML).

* **Workforce Scheduling:** Uses AI-powered engines to create compliant schedules that meet >90% of employee preferences while adhering to labor laws and contracts.
* **Demand Forecasting:** ML algorithms analyze historical data to predict staffing requirements (e.g., anticipating seasonal peaks in cleaning demand).
* **Travel Time Prediction:** ML improves the accuracy of travel time estimates for mobile teams, crucial for route optimization.
* **Agentic AI:** Exploring autonomous agents for supply chain optimization, indicating a forward-looking roadmap.

## 5. Risk Assessment

* **Algorithmic Bias:** Automated scheduling decisions must be monitored to ensure fairness in shift distribution.
* **Data Dependency:** Optimization quality is strictly tied to the quality of historical data; poor data leads to poor schedules.
* **Complexity & Learning Curve:** The system is enterprise-grade and requires expert configuration; it is not a "plug-and-play" tool for untrained planners.
* **Privacy:** Location tracking and performance monitoring raise employee privacy concerns that must be managed via policy.
